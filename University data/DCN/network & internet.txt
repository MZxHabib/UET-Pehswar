Network: A network is a group of connected devices, like computers or smartphones, that can talk to each other 
and share things like files and printers. It can be a small group of devices in a home or a larger group across different places.

Internet:
The internet is like a giant network that connects millions of computers and devices all around the world.
It allows people to access websites, send emails, watch videos, and do many other things online. 
It's like a huge collection of information and services that are available to anyone with an internet connection.

In short, a network is a smaller group of connected devices, while the internet is a big network that connects devices 
globally and provides access to a wide range of information and services.11
